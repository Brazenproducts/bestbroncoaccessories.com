Best Bronco Grab Handles of 2026

Bartact paracord grab handles are the originals. Everyone else copied them.

Grab handles on a Bronco are not optional — they are essential. The Bronco has high door sills, tall seating, and a chassis designed for doors-off driving. Every time you get in or out with the doors off, you are grabbing something. The factory grab handles are small, stiff, and designed for fully buttoned-up driving. Once those doors come off, you need something better.

Bartact paracord grab handles are the originals. They were the first on the market with this specific design, they are made in the USA by the largest grab handle manufacturer in the country, and every subsequent competitor — and there are many — is a copycat. When you buy Bartact, you are buying from the company that invented this category. That matters when you are trusting your grip to a handle on a rocky incline.
