Best Bronco Lift Kits of 2026

2" and 3" lift kits for better clearance, bigger tires, and the stance that makes a Bronco look like a Bronco.

A lift kit is one of the most transformative Bronco modifications you can make. Even a 2-inch lift improves approach and departure angles, clears larger tires, and gives the Bronco the stance it was always supposed to have. The 2021+ Bronco has excellent factory suspension geometry, which means a quality lift kit enhances what Ford already got right rather than fixing fundamental flaws.

2-Inch Lift Kits

1. Rough Country 2" Suspension Lift Kit

Editor's Choice — Best Value 2"

Rough Country 2" Suspension Lift Kit — Bronco (2021+)

★★★★★ 4.7/5 (best value for the money)
Budget to mid-range — excellent price-to-performance ratio

Rough Country is the go-to choice for Bronco owners who want a real lift without spending pro-level money. Their 2" kit uses quality components — struts, springs, and necessary hardware — to deliver a genuine suspension lift rather than a spacer. The ride quality is surprisingly good for the price, and the fitment on 2021+ Broncos is well-sorted at this point in the product lifecycle.

✓ Pros

  • Best price-to-performance ratio in the 2" category
  • Proper suspension lift — not just spacers
  • Good ride quality for the price
  • Clearance for 35" tires with minor trimming
  • Well-sorted 2021+ Bronco fitment at this point

✗ Cons

  • Not the same damping quality as Icon or Fox
  • Components are functional, not premium

2. Icon Vehicle Dynamics 2" Stage 3 Kit

Best Premium 2"

Icon Vehicle Dynamics 2" Stage 3 Suspension Kit

★★★★★ 4.9/5 (pro-level performance)
Premium — costs more, rides better

Their 2" Stage 3 kit includes their renowned 2.5" reservoir shocks that offer significantly better damping control than anything in the budget category. The ride quality improvement over a basic 2" kit is dramatic, especially on rough terrain.

  • Icon's legendary 2.5" reservoir shock quality
  • Dramatically better damping than budget options
  • Improved off-road performance without sacrificing street manners
  • Fully rebuildable components
  • Made in the USA

✗ Cons

  • Significantly higher price than Rough Country
  • Requires professional installation for best results

3" Lift Kits

3. Fox Performance Series 3" Lift Kit

Best 3" for Trail Performance

Fox Performance Series 3" Lift Kit — Bronco

★★★★★ 4.8/5
Mid to premium

Fox is one of the most recognized names in off-road suspension, and their Performance Series kit for the Bronco delivers a significant improvement in all conditions. The 3" height opens up room for 35" tires without extensive modification, and the Fox-tuned damping makes a real difference on rough terrain without sacrificing street comfort.

✓ Pros

  • Fox's renowned off-road damping technology
  • 3" of real height improvement
  • Clears 35" tires with the right wheel offset
  • Good balance of trail performance and daily drivability

✗ Cons

  • 3" lifts require more careful drivetrain angle consideration
  • May need extended brake lines and other supporting modifications

The Bottom Line

For the best value 2" lift: Rough Country is the correct answer. Icon Vehicle Dynamics is worth the premium if you want the best possible ride quality at 2". For 3" lifts: Fox Performance Series delivers the best trail-focused performance. Bilstein is the choice for owners who want excellent OE-quality damping for daily driving with occasional trail use. Rough Country 3" N3 remains the best budget path to a real 3" lift and 35" tire clearance.
